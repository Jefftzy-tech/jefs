Blood Wings

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

  • Type: Magic Item
  • Role: Burst Damage / Survivability
  • Cost: 3000 Gold
  • Release Date: Unknown

Description

Blood Wings grants an immense amount of magic power and increases survivability by converting magic power into a shield. It is ideal for mages who need both offensive and defensive boosts in late-game fights.

Stats

  • +175 Magic Power
  • +500 HP
  • Passive: Gain a shield equal to 200% of magic power

Lore

The Blood Wings are said to be born from the hearts of fallen archmages. Their pulsing crimson glow reflects the power and sacrifice required to wield such devastating force.

Notes

  • Best used in late-game scenarios for high damage output.
  • Provides excellent sustain through its massive shield.
  • Often the final core item in mage builds.
