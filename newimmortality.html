Immortality

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

  • Type: Defense Item
  • Role: Survival
  • Cost: 2120 Gold
  • Release Date: Unknown

Description

Immortality is a powerful defensive item in Mobile Legends that grants a second chance in battle. It revives the hero after death with a portion of HP and a shield, ideal for sustaining in team fights.

Stats

  • +800 HP
  • +40 Physical Defense
  • Passive: Resurrects hero 2.5 seconds after death with 15% HP and a shield

Lore

Forged from divine essence and hardened spirit, Immortality bestows the power to defy death itself. Warriors who wear it are feared not only for their strength but for their refusal to fall.

Notes

  • Best used on frontliners or fragile cores.
  • Provides strategic advantage in close team fights.
  • Has a cooldown after activation.
