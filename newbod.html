Blade of Despair

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

  • Type: Weapon
  • Role: Burst Damage
  • Cost: 3010 Gold
  • Release Date: Unknown

Description

Blade of Despair is one of the most powerful physical items in Mobile Legends. It greatly boosts physical attack and amplifies damage to enemies with low health.

Stats

  • +160 Physical Attack
  • +5% Movement Speed
  • Passive: Deals extra damage to enemies with less than 50% HP

Lore

This deadly blade was forged for pure destruction. Its edge never dulls, feeding on the despair of fallen enemies, making it a feared weapon on the battlefield.

Notes

  • Best used on physical burst heroes.
  • Very effective in finishing off low-HP enemies.
  • Often built as a late-game item.
