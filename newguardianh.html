Guardian Helmet

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

  • Type: Defense Item
  • Role: Regeneration
  • Cost: 2200 Gold
  • Release Date: Unknown

Description

Guardian Helmet is a defense item that greatly enhances a hero's HP and grants powerful HP regeneration when out of combat. It's especially effective for tanks and fighters in long battles.

Stats

  • +1550 HP
  • +20 HP Regen
  • Unique Passive: Recovers HP when out of combat

Lore

This ancient helmet was once worn by a titan guardian who never fell in battle. Its mystical aura continues to heal and protect its wearer from the most fatal blows.

Notes

  • Excellent for sustaining through lanes or jungle.
  • Recovery scales with max HP.
  • Perfect for heroes who disengage to heal quickly.
