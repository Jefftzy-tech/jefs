Flame Retribution

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

  • Type: Jungling Item
  • Role: Damage & Sustain
  • Cost: 630 Gold
  • Release Date: Unknown

Description

Flame Retribution enhances jungle clearing speed and provides life steal and burn damage on attacks to jungle monsters and enemies.

Stats

  • +40 Physical Attack
  • Unique Passive: Burn effect on attacks dealing extra true damage
  • Life Steal against jungle monsters

Lore

Forged in flames, this item grants the wielder the power to scorch enemies and sustain through jungle battles with intense heat.

Notes

  • Essential for junglers who rely on physical damage.
  • Helps to clear jungle camps faster and sustain HP.
  • Works well with physical damage heroes.
