Rapid Boots

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

  • Type: Movement Item
  • Role: Speed Boost
  • Cost: 800 Gold
  • Release Date: Unknown

Description

Rapid Boots increase the hero's movement speed and grant a burst of speed when moving out of combat, allowing quick repositioning or escapes.

Stats

  • +40 Movement Speed
  • Unique Passive: Movement Speed increases by 25% for 3 seconds when out of combat

Lore

These boots are crafted with lightweight materials and magical enchantments, allowing swift movement across battlefields and sudden dashes to outmaneuver enemies.

Notes

  • Best used for chasing or fleeing.
  • Great for junglers and assassins.
  • Passive helps in quick rotations.
