Bloody Retribution

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

  • Type: Jungling Item
  • Role: Damage & Lifesteal
  • Cost: 630 Gold
  • Release Date: Unknown

Description

Bloody Retribution increases jungle clearing speed and grants life steal and physical damage to help sustain in fights.

Stats

  • +40 Physical Attack
  • Unique Passive: Lifesteal effect on attacks
  • Bonus damage against jungle monsters

Lore

Forged with bloodlust, this item empowers the wielder with relentless power and the ability to recover health through fierce battles in the jungle.

Notes

  • Ideal for physical damage junglers.
  • Provides sustain during jungle farming and fights.
  • Increases damage to jungle monsters.
