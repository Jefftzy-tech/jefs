Athena's Shield

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

  • Type: Magic Defense
  • Role: Shield
  • Cost: 2150 Gold
  • Release Date: Unknown

Description

Athena's Shield is a magic defense item that provides a large shield and magic resistance. The shield regenerates when the hero takes magic damage, making it effective against magic damage dealers.

Stats

  • +900 HP
  • +56 Magic Defense
  • Unique Passive: Absorbs magic damage and regenerates shield

Lore

Forged from celestial materials, Athena's Shield protects its bearer from the most potent magical forces, granting them resilience and fortitude in the heat of battle.

Notes

  • Highly effective against magic burst heroes.
  • Shield regeneration helps sustain in team fights.
  • Best on tanks and fighters needing magic defense.
