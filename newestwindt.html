Wind Talker

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

  • Type: Attack Item
  • Role: Attack Speed & Critical
  • Cost: 1520 Gold
  • Release Date: Unknown

Description

Wind Talker increases attack speed and critical chance, allowing users to deal more damage rapidly with a chance of chain lightning strikes.

Stats

  • +40% Attack Speed
  • +20% Critical Chance
  • Unique Passive: Chain Lightning deals magic damage to enemies

Lore

The Wind Talker channels the speed and agility of the wind, striking enemies with swift and unpredictable lightning attacks.

Notes

  • Best for heroes relying on attack speed and critical hits.
  • Chain lightning can hit multiple targets.
  • Improves farming and team fight capabilities.
